I am currently a 4-th year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Tech, advised by Professor Matthieu R. Bloch. My current research interest involves Physical-layer Security, Quantum Information Theory, and Coding Theory. In particular, my current projects focus on designing the covert communication systems and its extension to bosonic systems (e.g., optical system). Prior to my graduate study, I was a research assistant advised by Professor Tzi-Dar Chiueh at National Taiwan University, working on the LDPC decoder with parallel programming.
